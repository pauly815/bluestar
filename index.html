Who We Are

Our focus on the Intelligence Community

Blue Star Software provides a range of IT support services to the US Intelligence Community (IC). We maintain a dedicated staff of highly-skilled software engineers and technical experts with current security clearances and training who can step onsite in days, not months. Whether you have an immediate requirement to extend your IC services team or you're looking to boost your surge capabilities, Blue Star can help.

Our highly-skilled technical staff

Our team of IT professionals are experts in a variety of disciplines, including big data, cloud, IT security, mobile, J2EE development, database administration, system architecture and engineering, and data center services.

Our responsiveness

Our ability to combine the right mix of technical skill and professional background to meet unique client requirements is part of the value you get when you work with Blue Star. Talk to us about the specific issues that face your enterprise and we will design a custom solution that meets your needs.

Our company culture

Our philosophy is to treat our employees well and provide them with greater visibility so they can make a difference. It’s simple, but it works. From our employee Intranet to our exceptional benefits package, we have created a unique environment to help our employees thrive.

Our competitive rates

Blue Star prides itself in providing top notch technical talent at competitive rates. We have done our best to keep our rates competitive across the board. We strive to keep overhead costs low to offer the best value to our customers who are operating within tight budget constraints.

What We Do

Big Data

We help the Intelligence Community leverage Big Data technologies to analyze extremely large datasets and deliver enhanced insights and analytics.

Cloud

As agencies deploy systems using private, public and hybrid cloud environments, we employ best practices to ensure that your cloud computing environment is a seamless, secure, high-performance solution.

Mobile

We help the IC harness mobile technologies to deliver access to services and information through smartphones and mobile devices.

Cybersecurity

We leverage cybersecurity tools and best practices to identify security assurance gaps, introduce processes to improve security while reducing risk, as well as monitor and manage the security program.
